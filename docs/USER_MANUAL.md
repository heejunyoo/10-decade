## 1. Getting Started

### Profile Setup
Upon first visit, you will be asked to create a **Profile** (e.g., "Dad", "Mom").
*   This helps the AI know *who* is asking questions.
*   This tracks your specific answers to Daily Interviews.

### ‚òÄÔ∏è The "Daily Memory"
On the dashboard, you will see a featured photo from the past.
1.  **The Question**: The AI will ask you a question about that moment.
    *   *Examples*: "Who cooked this meal?", "Why were you smiling?"
2.  **Answer**: Type your memory. This is saved forever attached to that photo.
3.  **Refresh (üîÑ)**: Don't like the question? Click the refresh icon to get a new one.

### ‚ûï Adding Memories
Click the **"Add Event"** or **"+"** button in the navigation bar.
*   **Upload**: Select photos or videos from your device.
*   **Wait**: The breakdown happens in the background. You can keep browsing.
    *   The AI will automatically find faces, date the photo, and write a caption.

## 3. Features

### üîç Search & Chat
Use the search bar at the top ("Search memories...").
*   **Semantic Search**: You don't need exact keywords. Try describing the scene:
    *   "Kids playing in the snow"
    *   "Red car in Busan"
*   **Chat Mode**: Ask follow-up questions to the results.

### üë• People Management
Go to the **People** tab to organize faces.
*   **Naming**: Click an "Unknown" face and give it a name. The system will auto-tag all future photos of that person.
*   **Merging**: If the system thinks "Dad" and "Unknown1" are different, you can merge them into one person here.

### üó∫Ô∏è Map View
See your journey geographically.
*   Pins appear automatically for photos with GPS data.
*   Click a pin to see all photos from that location.

### ‚è≥ Time Capsules
Leave a message for the future.
*   Choose a recipient (e.g., "My Daughter").
*   Set an **Open Date** (e.g., her 20th birthday).
*   The message remains **locked** and hidden until that specific date arrives.

## 4. Settings (Manage)

Access the **Manage** page to control the system.
*   **AI Provider**: accurate Local AI (Free/Private) or Cloud Gemini (Smarter).
*   **Theme**: Light / Dark / Auto.
*   **Backup**: Check backup status or trigger a manual backup.
