## 1. Installation (Mobile App)
This application is designed to live on your phone's home screen.

### iPhone (iOS)
1.  Open **Safari** and navigate to the website URL.
2.  Tap the **Share** button (Square with arrow up).
3.  Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**.
4.  Tap **Add**. You will see the Gold 'D' icon on your screen.

### Android (Chrome)
1.  Open **Chrome** and navigate to the website URL.
2.  Tap the **Menu** (three dots).
3.  Tap **"Add to Home screen"**.
4.  Confirm to install.

## 4. Adding Memories
1.  Tap the **"+" (Plus/Add)** button.
2.  **Gallery**: Select multiple photos/videos from your library.
3.  **Camera**: Tap "Take Photo" to capture a moment instantly.
4.  **Wait**: The AI will analyze the photo in the background (tags, mood, summary). You don't need to type anything!

---

## 5. Customization
Go to **Manage > System Configuration**:
*   **Design Theme**: Switch between `Classic` (Warm), `Nexus` (Dark/Cyber), `Forest` (calm), or `Retro`.
*   **AI Provider**: Choose between Local AI (Private) or Google Gemini (Cloud).

---

## 6. Tips
*   **Cinema Mode**: Turn your phone sideways (Landscape) in the Timeline to start a slideshow.
*   **Time Capsules**: You can write a letter that locks until a specific date. Great for birthdays!
